## High Concept

**Genre**: Action-RPG (creature collecting + Secret of Mana-style combat)
**Platform**: Mac & PC
**Engine**: Love2D (Lua)
**Tone**: Warm, hopeful, colorful — the joy of restoring light to a dim world

**Core loop**: Arrive in a shadowed region → explore and befriend locals → find the Beacon Shard → rekindle the Beacon Tower → watch the world flood with light → collect newly-revealed Lumins → move on.

**The central promise to the player**: *Every region you visit will transform from dim and foreboding to warm and alive. You will literally see the world become more beautiful as you progress.*
